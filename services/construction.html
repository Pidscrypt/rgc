  • Operational support in preparing well programs, materials utilization lists, drilling proposals.

    • Drilling Services

    For over Nine years, RGC has drilled, serviced and completed oil and gas wells for some of the most demanding clients such as OMV Yemen, E & P Petroleum, besides other international clients. The reliability of the group's services is evident in the retention of existing clients and continuous expansion of clientele.

    As a drilling Contractor, RGC operates a fleet of 20 drilling rigs and 50 workover rigs supported by extensive workshop and transportation facilities. The rigs are of modular design, 'Fit for Purpose' equipped with the latest technology such as PLC Controls and Top Drives.

    RGC has extensive experience in providing drilling and workover services. RGC has been in business providing drilling and oilfield services for more than 9 years and was involved in fire fighting in the Yemen oilfields .RGC is experienced in operating in urban areas with compact, sound insulated land drilling rigs having minimum effluent discharge. RGC has also developed in Oman, a lot of experience and expertise in operating mobile desert drilling rigs in remote desert locations backed with logistics, trucking, mobile camps with full maintenance and support structure systems.
