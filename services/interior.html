Process

Though each project is unique, we have a established a 6 Step process that ensures your project is completed on time

Space planning

Either refurbishment or newly built, we work on re-organising the layout in a neat and clear way. Providing the best opportunity for efficient spatial experience is the goal of this stage.

Concept Design

Non-orthodoz solutions and new ideas are the main outcomes of this stage. Each project have different circumstances, thus we provide optimum solutions in a creative manner.

Design Development

Several layers of design are usually required to turn the initial concept into a mature desgn. Material engineering, electro mechanical and other services are coordinated at this stage to deliver a well- studied project.

Documentaton I FFE & Specs

This stage is when the full packages of Constuction Documents and detailed specification sheets are prepared according to the design and each FF&E item is well documented with its full reference.

Procurement & Tender

Preparing request for tender and procurement sets the parameters and qualities required to execute our design as perfect as possible. This is done according to project’s budget, location and desired schedule.

Supervision

On site supervision and quality inspection is an important determinant of the dream coming true. Making sure the site is on track reduces any lost costs of back and forth construction works, and ensures the supreme quality of work delivered to our clients.
